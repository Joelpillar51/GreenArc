Arc., ESV. Martins Terzungwe Amough is a visionary architect and accomplished business leader driving innovation, operational excellence, and sustainable growth at the helm of Greenarc Global Consultants & Construction Ltd. Renowned for his strategic foresight and results-oriented leadership, he combines deep architectural expertise with robust project management and high-value construction delivery.
