Who We Are

Your Partner in Development

GREENARC GLOBAL CONSULTANTS & CONSTRUCTION LTD (GGCC) is a leading indigenous, multi-disciplinary firm specializing in Design, Construction, and Project Management solutions.

Established in 2009, we deliver innovative and cost-effective services across Building & Civil Engineering, Real Estate Development, Water & Electrical Engineering, Renewable Energy, Project Financing, and General Contracting.

With a team of experienced Architects, Engineers, and Project Managers, GGCC provides integrated, value-driven solutions tailored to client needs ensuring quality delivery, efficiency, and long-term satisfaction.

Through strong local and international partnerships, we execute projects to world-class standards, consistently delivering on time and within budget.
